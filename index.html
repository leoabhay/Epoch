About Epoch

Epoch is an atmospheric time-travel puzzle platformer that challenges you to think across dimensions. By shifting between the Past and the Future, you can manipulate objects, traverse broken bridges, and solve intricate environment puzzles.

How to Play
  • Move: A/D or ←/→
  • Jump: Space (or the jump button)
  • Time Shift: T (or the time button) — past is warm, future is cool
  • Puzzles: bridges, trees, switches, and movable boxes can differ per timeline
